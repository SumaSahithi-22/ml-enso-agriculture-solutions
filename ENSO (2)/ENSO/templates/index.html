ENSO Insight: Forecasting El Niño & La Niña

What is ENSO?

El Niño-Southern Oscillation (ENSO) is a climate phenomenon influenced by variations in the Walker Circulation. It affects global weather patterns by altering ocean temperatures and atmospheric pressure in the Pacific.
